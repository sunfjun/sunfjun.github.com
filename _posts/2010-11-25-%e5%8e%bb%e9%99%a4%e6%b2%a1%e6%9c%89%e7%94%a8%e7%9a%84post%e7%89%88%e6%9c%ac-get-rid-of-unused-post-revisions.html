Of course, do not forget to make a backup of your database before running the code. 只需要在你的Wordpress 数据库执行下面的sql语句。 所有的文章版本 都会 从你的数据库去除掉 
DELETE a,b,c
FROM wp_posts a
WHERE a.post_type = 'revision'
LEFT JOIN wp_term_relationships b
ON (a.ID = b.object_id)
LEFT JOIN wp_postmeta c ON (a.ID = c.post_id);
